Who All are getting Benefits?

Value Preposition

The predictive analysis fetched over the sensors along with the farm data are distributed among all the stakeholders for the following six benefits

Farmer-Customer Marketplace

Providing a marketplace where Farmers can sell their crops to FnC Mart and the consumers can purchase, giving them more market connectivity.

Farmer-Fertilizer Industry Supply

Connectivity of Farmer and fertilizer industries with the farm data in order to judge the right type of fertilizers that are needed for the farm

Farmer-Agriculture Department/GOI Policies

Farm Data will be shared with the Government, policies centering more on benefits the farmers can be formed.

Farmer-Bank Insurance/Loans

Shared data of farms with banks will provide ease to small farmers acquiring loans from the banks, data can be helpful for considering collaterals/guarantees.

Farmer-Transportation Facility

Connectivity of Farmer and transport companies, data shared will help the transportation companies to connect with the leads.

Farmer-Customer Transparency

The consumers can get details of all the transactions by scanning for details thereby validating its freshness.
